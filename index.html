Hi, my name is Alex. Currently I am working remotely as a freelance data analyst. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in commerce (Retail Management, TNOU - Chennai).

I have always enjoyed solving puzzles. So that’s how I look at big data sets: to me, it is one big puzzle I want to solve. Finding patterns nobody else sees is a challenge to me.

Before I started as a freelancer, I had worked for more than eight years as a webmaster and data analyst in consulting and e-commerce. I have worked for a couple of companies which gives me the ability to translate complex questions into understandable insights.
